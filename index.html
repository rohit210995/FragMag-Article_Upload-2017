Ripples

1. The articles can comprise of stories, poems, songs, etc. but must be based on the theme which is "Ripples".

2. The articles can written in English, Hindi or Marathi.

3. Please submit the articles as .doc or .docx file.

4. Hindi and Marathi articles can be hand-written in which case directly hand them over to any member of the Editorial team.

5. The Technical articles do not have to be in line with the theme and can be based on absolutely any topic.

6. You can also send your art- paintings, sketches, photographs, etc., however please make sure that the file size is less than 25 MB.

7. One can send multiple articles or art.

8. Please make a note that articles copied from any sources shall be rejected immediately as the Editorial team deploys a state-of-the-art plagiarism test.

9. If found guilty of plagiarism, no further articles shall be entertained.
